# Skydive Canopy Wingload Calculator

A modern web-based calculator for determining canopy wing loading in skydiving.

## What is Wing Loading?

Wing loading is a critical factor in skydiving that measures the amount of weight per square foot of your parachute canopy. It's calculated as:

**Wing Load = Total Weight (lbs) / Canopy Size (sq ft)**

Where Total Weight = Jumper Weight + Gear Weight

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Enter your body weight (without gear)
3. Enter your gear weight (rig, parachute, equipment)
4. Enter your canopy size in square feet
5. Click "Calculate Wingload" to see your result

## Wingload Categories

- **Low** (< 1.0 lbs/sq ft): Conservative, suitable for students
- **Moderate** (1.0 - 1.5 lbs/sq ft): Recreational skydivers
- **High** (1.5 - 2.0 lbs/sq ft): Experienced skydivers
- **Very High** (> 2.0 lbs/sq ft): Expert level, high performance
